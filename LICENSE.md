## 🔧 Troubleshooting & Common Issues

### ❌ The GUI doesn't appear after executing

- Confirm the script executed without errors — check your injector's output console.
- Try re-injecting after fully loading into the game world (not just the loading screen).
- Disable browser extensions or antivirus that might have modified the downloaded file.

### ❌ Auto Catch isn't working

- Make sure the toggle is enabled in the Offense tab of the GUI.
- Auto Catch requires the game to be in an active play state — it won't trigger in menus or pre-snap.
- If playing on mobile, confirm that your executor has overlay permissions granted.

### ❌ Script crashes on mobile

- Verify your device meets the minimum specs listed in the requirements section.
- Restart both the executor and Roblox and try again — avoid running other heavy apps simultaneously.
- Check if your executor version is up to date.

### ❌ Speed Boost causes rubber-banding

- Lower the `SpeedMultiplier` value. Anything above 2.5 may conflict with server-side physics checks.
- Enable `PingOptimizer` if it isn't already active.

### ❌ Hotkeys not responding on Mac

- macOS requires Accessibility permissions for global hotkeys. Go to **System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility** and add your executor.

### ❌ iOS sideloaded executor says "Untrusted Developer"

- Navigate to **Settings → General → VPN & Device Management**, find the developer profile, and tap "Trust."
